We believe the best founders
come with Canadian roots.
We find them first.

Nibi means "water" in Ojibwe — the language of the Indigenous people of the land we call Canada. Like water, the best founders find their own path, carving channels others don't see yet. Nibi is where that journey begins.

Our network runs deep across Waterloo, McGill, U of T, UBC, Queen's, and beyond. We meet founders in their dorm rooms, co-working spaces, and garages — long before Sand Hill Road comes calling.
