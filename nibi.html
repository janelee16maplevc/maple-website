Nibi.
Before Silicon Valley
finds you.

Nibi is Maple's accelerator for Canadian founders at the earliest stage — before the pitch is polished, before the term sheet arrives, before anyone else is paying attention.

We believe the best founders
come with Canadian roots.
We find them first.

Nibi means "water" in Ojibwe — the language of the Indigenous people of the land we call Canada. Like water, the best founders find their own path, carving channels others don't see yet. Nibi is where that journey begins.

Our network runs deep across Waterloo, McGill, U of T, UBC, Queen's, and beyond. We meet founders in their dorm rooms, co-working spaces, and garages — long before Sand Hill Road comes calling.

What Nibi Offers
01
First Check
Pre-seed capital from Maple before anyone else has conviction. We write the first check so you can build with confidence.
02
The Maple Network
Access to employee #1–25 at DoorDash, Flexport, Dropbox, Stripe, Figma, and more — people who built the companies you admire.
03
Silicon Valley Bridge
We help you make the jump. Introductions to top US investors, customers, and talent when the time is right.
04
Founder Thesis Support
We work through the 7 pillars of our investment thesis with you: Team, Tech, Timing, Monopoly, Distribution, Defensibility, Secret.
05
Canadian Community
Join a cohort of Canadian founders building at the frontier. Shared learnings, introductions, and a network that compounds over time.
06
Long-Term Partnership
We're not a 3-month program. Maple backs founders from pre-seed through IPO — we're in it for the full journey.
