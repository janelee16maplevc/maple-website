Our Approach

Our first network
is Canada

We find exceptional founders before they reach Sand Hill Road. Canada's universities — Waterloo, McGill, U of T, UBC, Queen's — produce world-class builders who arrive in Silicon Valley with a chip on their shoulder and something to prove. We're there first.

Uber Shopify OpenAI Databricks Cloudflare Roblox Notion Faire Moderna
$1T

Canadians have built companies worth over $1 trillion — Uber, Shopify, OpenAI, Databricks, Cloudflare, Notion, Roblox, Faire, Moderna. Our founding partner Andre Charoo was employee #25 at Uber and Co-Chair of The C100, the most influential network of Canadian expats in global tech.

We believe first. That's not a tagline — it's our investment posture. We write the first check, before the pitch is polished, before Silicon Valley is paying attention.
